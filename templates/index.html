Diagnostic Guidelines & Rules

The AI models evaluate radiographs based on standard anatomical guidelines. Here are the core classification rules used for diagnosis:

Elbow Classifications

Models detect fractures or anomalies in the humerus, radius, or ulna joints using ResNet50 joint-level features.

Hand & Wrist Classifications

Evaluates fractures within the carpals, metacarpals, or phalanges, cross-referencing patient demographics where relevant.

Shoulder Classifications

Evaluates glenohumeral alignments, clavicle, and scapular anomalies to identify dislocations and subluxations.
